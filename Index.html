This website presents a study of the game LIMBO.

LIMBO uses a monochromatic visual style to create a dark and atmospheric mood.

The game relies on environmental storytelling rather than dialogue.

Players must solve physics-based puzzles to progress through the world.

Trial and error is a core mechanic that teaches the player through failure.

Sound design and silence are used to increase tension and immersion.

The game encourages careful observation and slow, deliberate movement.
